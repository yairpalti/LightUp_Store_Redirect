From Crowd Lighting to Full Event Visuals

LightUp Crowd supports the full spectrum of event light shows, from plug-and-play activation to fully choreographed visual experiences.

Crowd Flash

Many different light patterns can be applied across the whole crowd or at random - such as blinking, twinkling, random or progressive - so you get energetic, high-impact effects that work in any venue with zero setup. The classic concert lighter, reimagined at scale.

Crowd Glow

Many different color and motion patterns can be applied - such as blinking, twinkling, random or progressive - so the crowd looks alive and dynamic rather than robotic. Each phone can follow the same pattern or vary subtly. Works in any space, any size, no seat mapping required.

Directed Glow

Same as Crowd Glow, with an operator triggering each stage live so the show stays in sync with the performance.

Zone Show

The crowd is divided into sections; each section glows in a different color simultaneously, creating sweeping patterns across the room.

Field Show

Location is detected automatically. Zones are driven by where each phone is. Outdoor only - ideal for stadiums and large open venues.

Canvas Show

Every seat individually addressed - the crowd becomes a high-resolution display for text, images, and video art.

Directed Canvas Show

Canvas Show with live operator control - full video art with cues triggered in real time.

Product Possibilities

Easy setup in advance

Configure your event and light show ahead of time. Endless options: colors, waves, blinks, text, images-all in exact sync. Fully cloud-based; no local operations or hardware.

Works offline - critical for big crowds

The show runs without internet at the venue. Once loaded, phones stay in sync by time. No venue Wi‑Fi or cellular needed. No expensive local hardware.

Optional operator on site

An operator uses their phone to control the light show sequence. No external expensive hardware-just a phone like everyone else.

Multiple events & tours

Run different events in different places or link several into one campaign. Multi-event effects: same pattern or different text/images per venue for a story or message.

No crowd size limit

Each phone works independently and is synced by time. No limit on crowd size-if it works for 2-3 phones, it works for the whole crowd.

Easy event setup & simulator

Define shows in minutes to hours. The simulator shows exact behaviour at the event so you can design and approve before go-live.

Custom & automatic for users

Easily customized per event-texts, colors, sequences. For users, once they're in the event, the app runs the show automatically. No buttons to press.

App Store & Google Play

Available on Apple App Store and Google Play. Multi-language support and ongoing improvements.
