🔦 Flashlight Basic Patterns

Non-Spatial Flashlight patterns

Each pattern below can be customized

Steady light Flashing Sparkle Special rhythms Building the crowd Fading the crowd
Steady light
1

Lights On

Everyone keeps their flashlight on — a solid, steady glow across the crowd.

2

Lights Off

Everyone turns their flashlight off — a moment of darkness.

Flashing
3

Slow Flash

All phones flash together at a relaxed, unhurried pace.

4

Medium Flash

A steady, moderate rhythm — faster than slow flash, still easy to follow.

5

Fast Flash

Quick, energetic flashing — adds excitement and movement.

Sparkle
6

Sparkle — Everyone

Every phone flickers at random moments, like a field of twinkling stars.

7

Sparkle — Just a Few

Only a small handful of phones sparkle while the rest stay dark — subtle and magical.

8

Sparkle — Mixed

Some phones sparkle while others stay on steadily — glitter over a warm glow.

Special rhythms
9

Strobe Bursts

Short bursts of very rapid flashes, then a pause — bold and high-energy.

10

Heartbeat

Two quick pulses in a row, like a heartbeat, then a gentle pause — emotional and rhythmic.

11

Breathing

Lights softly brighten and dim, like calm breathing — peaceful and organic.

12

Smooth Breathing

The same gentle rise and fall, but smoother and more even — a flowing wave of light.

Building the crowd
13

Grow — Steady

More and more phones turn on until the whole crowd is glowing — the audience builds together.

14

Grow — Flashing

The crowd grows phone by phone; each new light flashes as it joins in.

15

Grow — Sparkling

The crowd expands with a sparkling effect as new phones join — a shimmering build-up.

Fading the crowd
16

Fade Out — Steady

Lights gradually turn off until only a few remain — a gentle wind-down.

17

Fade Out — Flashing

The crowd slowly shrinks; remaining lights flash as phones drop out.

18

Fade Out — Sparkling

The crowd dwindles with a soft sparkle as phones go dark — a delicate ending.
